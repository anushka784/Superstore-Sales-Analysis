## 🎯 Project Objective
This project analyzes the Superstore dataset to provide actionable insights into retail operations. As an MBA student specializing in Finance and Operations, I focused on identifying high-margin product categories and regional sales trends to optimize business performance.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **Power BI:** Data Visualization & Dashboard Design.
* **Power Query:** Data Cleaning (Standardizing data types, handling nulls, and trimming text).
* **DAX:** Advanced measures for **Total Sales**, **Total Profit**, and **Profit Margin %**.
* **Data Modeling:** Implementation of a Star Schema with a dedicated Calendar Table.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
* **Operational Efficiency:** Maintained a healthy Profit Margin of ~12.5%.
* **Product Performance:** Technology remains the most profitable category despite lower transaction volume than Office Supplies.
* **Geographic Focus:** The West region consistently outperforms other regions in total sales revenue.
